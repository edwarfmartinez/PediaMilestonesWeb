Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how PediaMilestones handles information when you use the app. By using PediaMilestones, you agree to this policy.

PediaMilestones stores the milestone records and notes you create in the app. Depending on your device settings and platform services, limited technical data such as diagnostics, crash reports, and app performance information may also be processed to improve reliability.

How Information Is Used

Data is used to provide app functionality, help you track developmental milestones, and improve app performance and stability. Information is not sold to third parties.

Data Sharing

We may use service providers that support app hosting, analytics, or crash diagnostics. These providers process information only as needed to operate and improve the app.

Children's Privacy

PediaMilestones is intended for parents and caregivers. The app is not designed for direct use by children under 13 without parent or guardian involvement.

Data Security

Reasonable technical and organizational safeguards are used to protect information. No storage or transmission system can be guaranteed 100 percent secure.

Changes to This Policy
